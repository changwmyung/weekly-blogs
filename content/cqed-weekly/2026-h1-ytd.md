_The first four months of 2026 in cavity QED were defined by **two parallel storylines**: (i) polaritonic chemistry's continuing **reproducibility crisis** (VSC reaction-rate experiments failing to replicate; theory papers proposing concrete mechanisms or calling for reliability frameworks); and (ii) **first-principles cavity QED finally catching up** — a unified QED-DFT for solids, QED-coupled-cluster reformulations, and the experimental observation of **vacuum-dressed superconductivity in NbN**. Plus deep strong coupling realized in 3D Kane fermions, polariton supersolids and KPZ scaling, and chiral cavity enantioselectivity. This digest gathers 18 representative arXiv preprints from January through early May 2026, organized by theme._

---

## TL;DR

- **Polaritonic chemistry reckoning:** failed replications and refined ab initio mechanisms (the PTA S$_\mathrm{N}$2 case, the PTSB selectivity model, the chiral cavity enantioselectivity proposal) push the field toward mechanism-by-construction rather than effect-hunting.
- **First-principles cavity QED matures:** a **unified QED-DFT for solids** (electron-phonon-photon, all coupled), the coherent-state structure of QED-CC theory clarified, QED ring-CCD ↔ QED-RPA equivalence proven, and Fock-state cavity initialization wired into NEO real-time TDDFT.
- **Deep strong coupling realized** in 3D Kane fermions; ultrastrong Landau-polaritons in near-field-coupled split-ring resonators; **vacuum-dressed superconductivity in NbN** observed in a high-Q THz cavity (the field's most important experiment of the quarter).
- **Polariton many-body physics** continues to deliver: KPZ scaling explained, supersolids with Nambu-Goldstone modes, Kibble-Zurek + Kolmogorov scaling from single-shot vortex spectroscopy.
- **Cavity-modified chemistry beyond VSC:** **magnetic cavity QED** engineering of molecular PES via AFQMC, light-induced quantum self-trapping of vibrational excitons.

## 1. Polaritonic chemistry — the mechanism wars continue

### Toward Reliable Spectroscopic Analysis of Reaction Kinetics in Polaritonic Chemistry
[arXiv:2605.06002](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.06002) · physics.chem-ph · 2026-05-07
Frames the reproducibility problem directly: VSC reaction-rate experiments have sometimes failed to replicate, and the proposed mechanism (vibrational polariton formation) is contested. The paper builds toward a reliable theoretical framework for spectroscopic kinetics analysis under VSC — necessary infrastructure if polaritonic chemistry is to function as catalysis rather than as a 7-year debate.

### Vibrational strong coupling influences product selectivity in a model for post-transition state bifurcation reactions
[arXiv:2603.09713](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.09713) · physics.chem-ph · 2026-03-10
Cavity-coupled 2D PTSB model with a valley-ridge inflection point. Shows VSC reshapes the bifurcation outcome — a concrete mechanism for VSC product selectivity beyond hand-wavy "mode-specific" arguments. Useful because it's analytically tractable, so the prediction is falsifiable.

### Nucleophilic substitution at silicon under vibrational strong coupling: Refined insights from a high-level ab initio perspective
[arXiv:2601.18540](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.18540) · physics.chem-ph · 2026-01-26
PTA S$_\mathrm{N}$2 reaction under VSC reanalyzed with DFT + coupled-cluster. Identifies new encounter/product complexes, addresses the contested Si-C-stretching motion proposed as the VSC-active mode. The kind of careful mechanism-from-electronic-structure work the field has been short on.

### Two-dimensional IR-Raman spectroscopy of vibrational polaritons: Role of dipole surfaces
[arXiv:2603.24521](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.24521) · physics.chem-ph · 2026-03-25
2D IIR spectroscopy of liquid water under VSC computed from equilibrium-nonequilibrium cavity MD. Demonstrates that the dipole/polarizability surfaces must be *consistent with the underlying PES* — many earlier VSC spectroscopy calculations used inconsistent dipoles and got the wrong physics. A methodological wake-up call.

### Engineering molecular potential energy surfaces using magnetic cavity QED
[arXiv:2604.20969](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.20969) · physics.chem-ph · 2026-04-22
**Magnetic-cavity** coupling (not the usual electric-dipole) studied with AFQMC. Strong enough coupling makes H₂ ground state metastable and inverts the singlet-triplet gap; stabilizes symmetric ring geometries (Hₙ). A largely unexplored direction — most VSC/USC chemistry focuses on electric-dipole coupling.

### Quantum Dynamics of Enantiomers in Chiral Optical Cavities
[arXiv:2601.05643](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.05643) · physics.optics · 2026-01-09
**Chiral cavities under strong coupling lift the L/R enantiomer degeneracy** at the electronic-dipole level via parity-breaking field. Distinct site-energy shifts between enantiomers create robust enantioselective polariton states. If experimentally realizable, this is a route to enantioselectivity that bypasses traditional weak chiroptical effects.

## 2. First-principles cavity QED — the methods are arriving

### Unified ab initio QED-DFT for cavity-modified electron-phonon-photon coupling in solids
[arXiv:2603.24095](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.24095) · cond-mat.mtrl-sci · 2026-03-25 ⭐
The breakthrough paper of the quarter for ab initio cavity QED. **Self-consistent QEDFT for periodic solids** with collective light-matter coupling, density functional perturbation theory for phonons, and treatment of polarization/optical response — all unified. Previous QEDFT work was atom/molecule-limited; this opens cavity-modified solid-state computation in earnest.

### Spin-Flip Configuration Interaction for Strong Static Correlation in QED
[arXiv:2603.18228](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.18228) · physics.chem-ph · 2026-03-18
Spin-flip CI extended to QED settings, handling strong static correlation (conical intersections, bond breaking) under cavity coupling. Where TDHF/TD-DFT break (single-determinant failure), SF-CI in QED gets the topology right.

### The coherent-state transformation in QED-CC theory
[arXiv:2602.18391](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.18391) · physics.chem-ph · 2026-02-20
Reformulates QED coupled-cluster from the perspective of the coherent-state transformation's non-vanishing commutator with the polaritonic cluster operator. The cleaner formal structure should make QED-CC less brittle to numerical implementation — an issue several groups have noted.

### Cavity QED Ring Coupled Cluster and the Random Phase Approximation
[arXiv:2602.09968](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.09968) · physics.chem-ph · 2026-02-10
Generalizes the well-known particle-hole RPA ↔ ring-CCD equivalence to the QED case. **QED-RPA ≡ QED-ring-CCD** numerically. Useful both as a theoretical bridge and as a faster route to QED correlation energies (RPA is cheaper than full QED-CCD).

### Initialization with a Fock State Cavity Mode in Real-Time NEO Polariton Dynamics
[arXiv:2602.11335](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.11335) · physics.chem-ph · 2026-02-11
Mean-field-quantum and full-quantum real-time NEO-TDDFT for VSC, initialized with photon number states. Addresses the question of when quantum vs classical electrodynamics matter for polariton dynamics — and produces simulation tooling that handles both.

## 3. Coupling regimes: deep strong, ultra strong, vacuum-only

### Deep Strong Light-Matter Coupling in 3D Kane Fermions
[arXiv:2604.28042](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.28042) · cond-mat.mes-hall · 2026-04-30
**Deep strong coupling** — interaction exceeds the bare frequencies — realized in 3D Kane fermion systems. Ground state features strong photon-matter correlations and the long-debated **superradiant phase transition** with spontaneous dipole ordering and photon condensation becomes accessible. A long-standing controversy with a now-concrete platform.

### Ultrastrong light-matter coupling in near-field coupled split-ring resonators
[arXiv:2601.19292](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.19292) · cond-mat.mes-hall · 2026-01-27
Photocurrent spectroscopy of cyclotron-resonance Landau polaritons in **near-field-coupled split-ring resonators** — a regime previously unexplored after a decade of SRR-based USC work. Includes a topological SRR configuration; finds USC distinct from the usual far-field SRR coupling.

### Cavity Control of Strongly Correlated Electrons Beyond Resonant Coupling
[arXiv:2603.18933](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.18933) · quant-ph · 2026-03-19
Non-perturbative calculation of cavity-induced modification of the magnetic exchange interaction J in the half-filled Hubbard model — including the full cavity mode structure and first-principles parameters. **Vacuum modification of strong-correlation physics is real and computable**; the off-resonant nature is the key (correlated electrons lack a characteristic energy scale, so resonant analyses miss the effect).

### Vacuum-dressed superconductivity in NbN observed in a high-Q terahertz cavity
[arXiv:2601.08191](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.08191) · physics.optics · 2026-01-13 ⭐
**The experimental headline of the quarter.** NbN thin films inside high-Q THz photonic-crystal cavities show modified superconducting response — *without external driving*, from quantum vacuum fluctuations alone. The first credible experimental demonstration that vacuum-only cavity coupling can modify a material property of fundamental interest (superconductivity). Validates a class of theoretical predictions and opens an experimental program.

## 4. Polariton condensates and many-body physics

### Collective Excitations and Stability of Nonequilibrium Polariton Supersolids
[arXiv:2604.21353](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.21353) · cond-mat.mes-hall · 2026-04-23
Spectrum of collective excitations of polariton supersolids; demonstrates Nambu-Goldstone modes from spontaneously broken phase + translation symmetries. Builds on the Trypogeorgos et al. 2025 Nature observation and clarifies the role of attractive polariton interactions in stabilizing the phase.

### The origin of KPZ-scaling in arrays of polariton condensates
[arXiv:2604.00905](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.00905) · cond-mat.mes-hall · 2026-04-01
**Mechanism explanation** for KPZ universal scaling observed in driven-dissipative polariton condensates: the key is *Goldstone mode population fluctuations* from spontaneously broken U(1). Numerical simulations + analytical theory match the observed exponents. A satisfying closure on the "why KPZ here" question.

### Single-Shot Flow Spectroscopy of a Polariton Condensate: Kibble-Zurek and Kolmogorov-Like Scaling
[arXiv:2601.22719](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.22719) · cond-mat.mes-hall · 2026-01-30
Single-shot interferometric vortex tomography in a room-temperature organic polariton condensate. **Kibble-Zurek scaling** in vortex number with pump power; **Kolmogorov-like scaling** in the incompressible flow component. Direct vortex-resolved measurement makes this one of the cleanest polariton-condensate non-equilibrium universality demonstrations.

## Trends & themes

A few patterns from the quarter:

- **Polaritonic chemistry is in a reckoning.** The VSC reaction-rate-modification field is split between (i) refined ab initio mechanism papers carefully recomputing past systems and (ii) papers calling for reliability frameworks. Expect the next two quarters to look more like systematic theory-experiment iteration than the past mode of "we see an effect, here's a story."
- **First-principles cavity QED has gone solid-state.** The unified QED-DFT for solids is the most important methods paper of the quarter; expect a wave of cavity-modified-materials applications building on it.
- **Vacuum-only modification is no longer hypothetical.** The NbN experiment provides ground truth for years of theoretical predictions about cavity-modified superconductivity, magnetism, and correlation. The next obvious experiments: SrTiO₃, La₂CuO₄, twisted bilayer graphene.
- **Deep strong coupling exits the proposal era.** With 3D Kane fermions identified as a platform and SRR engineering pushing further into USC, the regime where coupling exceeds bare frequencies is now within experimental reach.
- **Polariton condensates: the universal scaling lab.** KPZ, supersolids, Kibble-Zurek, Kolmogorov — the polariton platform is delivering clean realizations of universality classes that are hard to reach in other condensed-matter systems.
